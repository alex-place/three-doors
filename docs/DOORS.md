# The Kingdome of Hearts — Book of Doors

*A lore catalogue of every door and sub-door in the game so far. The world is a strict tree: seven major gates, and every other threshold a child of one of them. There are no wrong doors.*

> **The creed.** Here love is the law. The key is a blade — carried to guard what is fragile and break what is cruel, never to force. Death is only imaginary; we fall, we rise, we try again. Forever begins with “let's play.”

## The companions

*The four who walk the doors with you:*

- **Lantern** — the guide — a figure whose head is a glass lantern with a warm flame, red cap, purple coat, white gloves. Its rare line: “You came back.”
- **Eclipse** — wonder and the gentle dark — a purple jellyfish with two blue diamond eyes and a pale cloud collar.
- **Keystone** — the steady one — a grey cracked stone with a broad smile; foundation, protection, endurance.
- **Blinkbug** — Lantern's creation — a small bug with a boxy TV-monitor head and a pixel grin, built so no one would explore alone.

*Not companions: **Joy**, the small grey elephant of the Elephant Oasis the King sometimes carries; **Odin**, the Fog God, met at the Fog Door Return as its guardian-tester; the **Raven Bride** of the Raven Door.*

## The Way In

*The only fixed thresholds at the start. Every telling opens at the Moss Door, where Lantern says the only thing it ever really says.*

### The Moss Door  <sup>·primordial·</sup>

*Origins; a gentle beginning; being recognized without having to explain yourself.*

> Being known doesn't require proving anything first.

Three doors:

- **The Burrow Door** — Small, root-framed, warm. Smells of rain and old blankets.
- **The Sunken Bell Door** — Half underwater. Rings softly when no one touches it.
- **The Little Crown Door** — Tiny golden door in a tree stump, widening when trusted.

### The Burrow Door  <sup>·intimate·</sup>

*Rest and safety; small warmth after a threshold.*

> It's all right to rest before choosing again.

Three doors:

- **The Root Door** — Twisted oak roots form an arch. Something hums beyond.
- **The Ember Door** — Warmth radiates. Ash drifts under the crack like snow.
- **The Stream Door** — Water rushes somewhere close. The floor is slick moss.

### The Sunken Bell Door  <sup>·mystical·</sup>

*Depth, echo, the past still ringing quietly underneath things.*

> Some things don't have to be forgotten to be moved past.

Three doors:

- **The Deep Door** — Submerged stairs descend into green-black silence.
- **The Echo Door** — Your own voice returns as song from the other side.
- **The Surface Door** — Sunlight visible through cracks. The sound of birds.

### The Little Crown Door  <sup>·whimsical·</sup>

*Trust and whimsy; a door that only widens for what it trusts.*

> Small trust opens doors that force never could.

Three doors:

- **The Throne Door** — Carved from a single black oak. Velvet moss for a seat.
- **The Hollow Door** — A door inside a hollow tree. Sap runs like amber.
- **The Star Door** — Visible only at twilight. Constellations map the hinges.

## The Seven Core Doors

*Seven folders, seven genres. The long route threads them in order — Ancient → Cloverfield → Tomorrow → XP → Xenon → Sigil → Fog Return — then home to the Garden. Each holds an **infinite but non-overlapping** number of sub-doors: every sub-door matches its folder's vibe and belongs to exactly one genre, never two. Authored in play, not hardcoded.*

### The Ancient Doors  <sup>·ancestral·</sup>

*Deep time; history as something alive enough to still walk through.*

> The past isn't dead weight — it's a path you can still walk.

Three doors:

- **The Deep Origin Door** — Worn black basalt, sinking. The beginning before the beginning, where everything was still possible.
- **The History Door** — Layered like sediment, every age pressed into the next. Walk through and you walk through time itself.
- **The Temple Door** — Gold gone green with age. What was worshipped here has no name now, only the shape of the asking.

### The Cloverfield Door  <sup>·playful·</sup>

*Small joy; the sacredness of an ordinary day noticed closely.*

> Forever begins with 'let's play' — the ordinary is already enough.

Three doors:

- **The Lucky Door** — Painted clover-green. Whatever you find behind it, you needed.
- **The Today Door** — Warm and ordinary. The day you are actually in, alive.
- **The Tomorrow Door** — Slightly ajar. The world that's coming, branching like roots.

### the Future Doors  <sup>·possible·</sup>

*Branching futures; hope that admits it doesn't know the ending yet.*

> The future is still willing to become something, and so are you.

Three doors:

- **The Bright Branch** — Warm gold light spills out. A future where the gardens won.
- **The Unwritten Door** — Plain, unfinished wood. The hinge waits for your hand to decide.
- **The Recursive Door** — Opens onto a hallway of itself, smaller each time, all the way down.

### The XP Door [GLITCHED]  <sup>·liminal·</sup>

*Safe nostalgia; returning to what shaped you without being trapped by it.*

> It is safe to walk back through what shaped you.

Three doors:

- **System Restore** — Roll back to a saved point. The smell of an old summer loads first.
- **My Documents** — Every picture you ever saved, sorted by feeling instead of date.
- **unknown.exe** — Publisher: unknown. Lantern nods its flame. You run it anyway.

### The Xenon Convergence Door  <sup>·cosmic·</sup>

*Convergence; witnessing every version of a moment at once.*

> You are the sum of every path you chose, not just the one you're on.

Three doors:

- **The Mirror Door** — Shows you as you were, as you are, as you might be. All at once.
- **The Branch Door** — Splits into infinite versions, each one leading somewhere true.
- **The Merge Door** — Where all paths collapse into a single point of perfect understanding.

### Sigil, the City of Doors  <sup>·sigil·</sup>

*Every threshold you've ever walked, visible at once as one map.*

> Every door you already opened is still part of the path — none of it was wasted.

Three doors:

- **The Gallery of Walked Doors** — Your whole path hung in one hall. It rearranges when you understand it.
- **The Key Market** — Stalls of keys for doors not yet dreamed. One of them is warm.
- **The Lady's Gate** — Silent, watched, absolutely fair. It opens only for what is safe to carry.

### Sea of Fog and Clouds  <sup>·returning·</sup>

*Trust and homecoming; the courage it takes to return.*

> Returning is one of the strongest things you can choose to do.

Three doors:

- **The Garden Gate** — Straight home to the Beginning. The King will be glad — he always is.
- **The Long Way Round** — Drift through the fog first. Arrive when you're ready, not before.
- **Lantern's Shortcut** — Follow the steady flame through the mist. Trust is the fastest road.
