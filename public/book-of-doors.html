The Companions

Who walks the doors with you. The way Alex draws them by hand is the truth; a picture that disagrees is the picture that's wrong.

  • Lantern — the guide — a figure whose head is a glass lantern with a warm flame; red cap, purple coat, white gloves. Its rare line: “You came back.”
  • Eclipse — wonder, and the gentle dark — a purple jellyfish with two blue diamond eyes and a pale cloud collar.
  • Keystone — the steady one — a grey cracked stone with a broad smile; foundation, protection, endurance.
  • Blinkbug — Lantern's own creation — a small bug with a boxy monitor head and a pixel grin, built so that no one would ever have to explore alone.

Not companions: Joy, the small grey elephant of the Elephant Oasis the King sometimes carries · Odin, the Fog God, met at the Fog Door Return as its guardian-tester.

The Seven Core Doors

Seven folders — seven genres. The long route threads them in order — Ancient → Cloverfield → Tomorrow → XP → Xenon → Sigil → Fog Return — then home to the Garden. Each holds an infinite but non-overlapping number of sub-doors: every sub-door matches its folder's vibe and belongs to exactly one genre, never two. They're authored in play, not hardcoded.
